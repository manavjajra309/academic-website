About Us

PhotoSpiMedx Pvt. Ltd. is a MedTech startup developing affordable, portable solutions for early disease detection. Using fluorescence spectroscopy, optical sensing, and AI-driven analytics, we create Fluorescence-based devices for rapid, non-invasive screening. Our mission is to make diagnostics accessible, accurate, and real-time, especially in resource-limited settings, improving early diagnosis and patient outcomes.
